Take A Few Moments

This is box breathing. Taking time out of our day to gently focus on our breathing is beneficial.

To begin, sit comfortably.

Follow the circle and trace the square with your eyes.

Breathe in slowly through your nose when the circle is at the top for four seconds.

Then hold for four as the circle follows the right side.

Breathe out slowly through your mouth when the circle is at the bottom for four.

Then hold again for four as the circle follows the left side.

Repeat this breathing exercise for as long as you wish.

Enjoy your stay.
